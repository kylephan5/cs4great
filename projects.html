About the Project: The Council on Aging of Elkhart County (COA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting senior citizens by providing life-giving services. They provide free transportation, social activities, financial assistance, and in-home medical care. One of their newer services is a collection of contacts for nearby resources that can help these individuals. Previously, seniors had to call the COA phone number and an employee either read off the appropriate results by filtering an Excel spreadsheet or sent screenshots back over email. This process was becoming cumbersome and inefficient for COA, so they reached out to CS for Good and asked for a modification to their website that would allow for this process to be automated. Since then, we have added a new page to their website for the “Resource Library” using Squarespace, Airtable, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It includes a form that the seniors can fill out with a category and optional filters for the query. Upon submission, the senior can either see the results in the web browser or print out the result table. The results of this addition have been overwhelmingly positive with a 30% increase in traffic to the website as a whole. In addition, the Resource Library page has become the second most popular page on the website, behind only the home page, and has the lowest bounce rate of any page. The Council on Aging of Elkhart County is doing great work for the seniors in the local community and this modification will continue to help them reach a larger and broader audience.

About the Project: The Forever Learning Institute CS4Good Project's goal is to teach an engaging class about Internet Safety and Communication to senior citizens in the South Bend area. The class provides them with information on how to stay safe from scams and fake information as well as keeps them updated on the newest technologies for staying connected with others online. We also help students after class with their own technology issues and questions. For each of the classes we decide the topics and activities and create slides on Canva. The group teaches once a week in person at the Forever Learning Institute in South Bend.
