{Our Mission}

Our Mission: CS4Good's aim is to use computing technology as a tool to create a positive social impact and a culture of social engagement.

Who We Are: Computer Science for Good (CS4Good) is a student-led club at the University of Notre Dame.

What We Do: We work on projects that exist at the intersection of technology and social good by partnering with local non-profits and assisting them with any technological needs they might have.
